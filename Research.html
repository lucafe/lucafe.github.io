Research Interests

My research interests focus on pure and applied mathematics, and include:

  • Science communication and Effective Graphical Display
  • Mathematics and Physics Education
  • Mathematical Modeling, Simulation and Computation.
  • Data Science
  • Uncertainty Quantification, Statistics, and Probability.
  • Optimization, Control Theory, and Machine Learning.
  • Approximation Theory, Integration Methods and Model Order Reduction.
